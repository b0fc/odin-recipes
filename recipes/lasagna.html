Lasagna



Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan
  • Egg
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water per the recipe below.
  2. Step 2: Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Step 3: Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Step 4: Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
